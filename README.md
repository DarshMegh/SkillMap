# 🚀 SkillMap AI

SkillMap AI is an AI-powered personalized learning roadmap generator that creates structured learning paths based on your goal, current experience level, and target timeline.

Unlike generic roadmaps, SkillMap AI generates a custom plan tailored specifically to your learning journey.

---

## ✨ Features

- 🎯 Personalized AI-generated learning roadmaps
- 📚 Supports any learning goal (Web Development, AI, DSA, UI/UX, etc.)
- 📈 Experience-based recommendations
  - Complete Beginner
  - Beginner (Some Basics)
  - Intermediate
  - Advanced
- ⏳ Flexible timelines
  - 1 Month
  - 3 Months
  - 6 Months
  - 1 Year
- ⚡ Fast roadmap generation
- 💻 Clean and responsive dark-themed interface
- 🤖 AI-powered content generation using Claude API

## 📸 Preview

### AI Roadmap Generation

## ⚙️ How It Works

1. Enter the skill you want to learn.
2. Select your current experience level.
3. Choose your desired completion timeline.
4. Click *Generate My Roadmap*.
5. SkillMap AI creates a personalized roadmap with structured milestones and recommended learning resources.

## 🌟 Why SkillMap AI?

Most learning platforms provide the same roadmap to everyone.

SkillMap AI creates personalized learning paths based on:

- Your goal
- Your experience
- Your available time

This makes learning more structured, efficient, and achievable.
